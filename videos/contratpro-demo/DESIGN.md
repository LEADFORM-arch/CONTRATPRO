# ContratPro Demo Visual System

## Style Prompt

Premium SaaS demo for a CVC maintenance contract cockpit. The visual world is a dark executive control room: zinc-black surfaces, navy steel panels, diagnostic cyan for AI reading, energy green for recurring revenue secured, amber for commercial friction, and crisp white text. The video should feel like a founder-led product demo for chauffagistes: field-aware, commercially sharp, calm under pressure, and focused on contracts that must not sleep in Excel.

## Colors

- Control black `#05070A`: background and deep product surfaces.
- Navy steel `#0F172A`: screenshot frames and structured panels.
- Diagnostic cyan `#38BDF8`: Architecte IA, signal reading, analysis lines.
- Energy green `#34D399`: revenue secured, next action, validation.
- Caution amber `#F59E0B`: forgotten renewals, objections, friction.
- Zinc white `#F4F4F5`: primary text and decisive labels.

## Typography

- Inter for all UI, captions, and data labels.
- Heavy weights for decisions, tabular numbers for KPIs, compact uppercase for metadata.

## Motion

- Smooth control-room motion: slide, wipe, parallax, pulse on data signals.
- Screenshots enter as framed product evidence, not floating marketing cards.
- Transitions use dark wipes and diagnostic scan lines between scenes.

## What NOT to Do

- No generic blue SaaS gradients.
- No beige, purple-led, or decorative orb background.
- No fake UI replacing the captured SaaS screenshots.
- No playful mascot or cartoon explanation.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
